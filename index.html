[ FAQ ]

Common questions.

What does Cinexa Studio offer?+

Cinexa Studio is a full post-production house offering offline edit, colour grading, sound design, VFX and motion graphics under one roof, handled by the same team from first cut to final delivery.

Where is Cinexa Studio based?+

Cinexa Studio is based in Pune, Maharashtra, and works with productions across India.

What kind of projects does Cinexa Studio take on?+

Narrative films, documentaries, brand and corporate films, music videos, and short-form social content and reels.

How do I start a project with Cinexa Studio?+

Reach out over email, phone or WhatsApp with your footage details, timeline and brief — the studio replies with next steps, usually within 24 hours.
